Step 1: What is a Two-Way Table?

A two-way table helps us organize data about two different categories. For example, we can survey students about whether they ride a bike AND whether they ride a skateboard.

The rows show one category (riding a bike), and the columns show another (riding a skateboard). The "Total" cells show the sum of each row or column. Let's practice filling one out!
